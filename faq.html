Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Q1: What is the history of Porsche?

A: Porsche was founded in 1931 by Ferdinand Porsche. The company initially offered vehicle development work and consulting but began producing cars in 1948 with the introduction of the Porsche 356. Over the decades, Porsche has become renowned for its high-performance sports cars, including the iconic 911, and has expanded its lineup to include SUVs and electric vehicles.

Q2: Where are Porsche cars manufactured?

A: Porsche cars are primarily manufactured in Germany. The main production facilities are located in Stuttgart and Leipzig. The company ensures that each vehicle meets the highest standards of quality and performance.

Maintenance and Service

Q6: How often should I service my Porsche?

A: It is recommended to follow the maintenance schedule outlined in your Porsche’s owner’s manual. Generally, a service check should be performed every 10,000 to 15,000 miles or once a year, whichever comes first. Regular maintenance ensures your Porsche performs at its best.

Q8: What warranty does Porsche offer?

A: Porsche offers a 4-year/50,000-mile new car limited warranty, which includes 24-hour roadside assistance. Additionally, there is a 12-year long-term warranty against rust perforation on all new Porsche vehicles. For more details, visit our Warranty page.
