About Us

Our Mission

At Porsche, our mission is to deliver unparalleled driving experiences and to continuously set new standards in the automotive industry. We are committed to innovation, excellence, and sustainability, ensuring that each Porsche embodies the perfect blend of performance, luxury, and efficiency.

Our History

Porsche was founded in 1931 by Ferdinand Porsche. Initially focused on vehicle development and consulting, the company began producing its own cars in 1948 with the Porsche 356. The launch of the Porsche 911 in 1964 marked the beginning of an era, establishing Porsche as a leader in high-performance sports cars. Over the decades, Porsche has expanded its lineup to include SUVs, sedans, and electric vehicles, all while maintaining its core values of performance and innovation.

Our Values

We are driven by a passion for excellence and a commitment to pushing the boundaries of what is possible. Our core values include:

  • Innovation: We continuously strive to lead the industry with groundbreaking technologies and forward-thinking design.
  • Performance: Every Porsche is engineered to deliver an exhilarating driving experience, combining speed, agility, and precision.
  • Quality: We uphold the highest standards of craftsmanship and reliability in every aspect of our work.
  • Sustainability: We are dedicated to reducing our environmental impact through sustainable practices and the development of electric and hybrid vehicles.
  • Customer Focus: Our customers are at the heart of everything we do. We are committed to providing exceptional service and creating lasting relationships with our Porsche family.
